Johann Brehmer

I am a physicist turned machine learner. I work as a research scientist at CuspAI, working on machine learning--driven discovery of materials for carbon capture.

Generally I am interested in solving problems with a mix of machine learning and scientific domain knowledge, for instance in the form of simulators, geometry, or causal structure. Before CuspAI, I spent time at Qualcomm AI Research, at NYU in Kyle Cranmer's lab, at Heidelberg University in Tilman Plehn's group, and at Imperial.
